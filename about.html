About Rugby123

Rugby123 - Start Playing Rugby Today! is an independent rugby information publishing company located on Rugby Road in downtown Baltimore, Maryland. The company publishes information about how to play rugby to help beginners who want to start playing today. Although rugby is played in various countries around the world, this site contains information about rugby union gameplay in the United States. Thus, this site is targeted toward people in the U.S. who are interested in learning about the sport.

The sport of rugby is believed to have originated in 1823 at the Rugby School of Rugby, England when William Webb Ellis took and ran with the ball. Although there is no solid evidence to support these origins, the international committee named the Rugby World Cup the “William Webb Ellis Trophy.”

Rugby is a team sport played with an oval ball (larger than a football), which can be carried, passed, or kicked. The sport is sometimes described as a mix between American football, which was derived from the sport of rugby, and soccer. However, unlike the two sports, rugby is a continuous, non-stop game played without timeouts or substitutions.

In the U.S., the typical rugby seasons are in the spring and fall. Training for the spring season generally starts in late January or early February, with games running from early March to early or mid-June. Training for the fall season usually commences in early August, with games running from late August or early September to early November. Oftentimes, 7s tournaments occur during the summer season. (See Game Overview for more about 7s.) However, Rugby123 publishes its content on the Internet to make it available to users 24/7/365, not just during the primary rugby seasons.

The company's main mission is to help people understand the basics of rugby. Rugby123's vision is to promote positive conversation about rugby, as well as to increase the size of the rugby community nationwide. Some of the company's primary goals are to:

  1. Provide thorough information to enable people to start playing rugby today!
  2. Motivate people to get involved in the rugby world.
  3. Be recognized as the lead source of online information for beginning rugby players (aka ruggers).
  4. Maximize its reputation as a high quality, accurate information provider.
  5. Increase the number of subscribers across the U.S.
  6. Maintain its 24-hour response rate guarantee.
  7. Expand the number of tutorials offered.

Rugby123 - Start Playing Rugby Today! was founded by former rugby player, Rachel Armington. Rachel began her rugby career in 2009 as a freshman at Syracuse University. In fall 2010, she transferred to the University of Maryland, College Park, where she continued playing for the Maryland Terrapins. While at Maryland, she also played for a brief time for the PRU. Rachel's primary positions were inside center and outside center.
