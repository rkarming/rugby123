Try, Try, Try Again!

You have the ball, but you do not have any passing options. You must gain ground by running with the ball and dodging your opponents' tackles. How long can you run with the ball before getting tackled?

Use the navigational buttons to move the rugby ball (you) up, down, left, and right.
Note: you can press and hold the buttons to move!
The goal is to dodge the pink blocks (your opponents' tackling zones) and carry the ball for as many seconds as possible. Click on the pause button to pause the game. (Once paused, a resume button will appear.) Click "Start Playing!" when you are ready to play! Good luck!

Click "Resume" to continue playing!
