Links

The table below contains links to other websites that may be of interest to people who want to learn more about the sport of rugby union.

Helpful Resources for More Rugby Information
Resource Description Topic(s)
A Beginner's Guide to Rugby Union This resource, by World Rugby, educates players, coaches, match officials, and unions about rugby by providing them with reading materials, videos, and an online test to check their knowledge. Comprehensive
How To Do a Rugby Tackle This 2:31 YouTube video provides a tutorial on how to perform a rugby tackle. Tackling
Kickology This site includes five instructional kicking videos featuring known rugby players and coaches. Kicking
LineoutCoach This site, by Gavin Hickie, rugby coach for Dartmouth College and USA Rugby, gives detailed information on the rugby positions. Positions
Rugby Coach Weekly This site offers rugby drills and coaching advice to improve performance and skills. Comprehensive
Rugby Passing Technique This 9:54 YouTube video provides rugby drills to improve passing technique. Passing
RugbyFootballHistory.com This resource explains the history of rugby union in areas such as scoring, laws, positions, and equipment. Comprehensive
RugbyHow This site explains the basic rules of rugby, the players and positions, and skills such as catching, passing, and kicking. Comprehensive
Talk Rugby Union This site discusses the basics of rugby union and includes information on lineouts, scrums, rucks and mauls, offsides, passing, tackling, kicking, and positions. Comprehensive
TeachPE.com This site includes videos and descriptions of various rugby passes, as well as drills to help improve your passing skills. Comprehensive
UMD Women's Rugby This site, by the University of Maryland, College Park women's rugby club team, provides information on the rugby positions, as well as on scrums and lineouts. Lineouts, Positions, Scrums
World Rugby: Laws of Rugby Union On this site, World Rugby publishes the official laws for the sport of rugby union. Laws
USA Rugby is the national governing body for rugby in America.
